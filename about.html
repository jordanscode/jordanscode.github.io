Jordan Staniscia is a designer building digital products & services in San Francisco.

Currently, Jordan is the founding designer at you.com, a new product search engine. In his spare time he advises companies like Culdesac.

Before that, Jordan was the lead designer of customer experience at Instacart, that company you might already get your groceries from every week. He's also worked at at Abstract helping improve how designers actually design by adding modern technology to their tools and Storify which helped journalists tell stories with social media as platforms like Twitter and Facebook became newsworthy for the first time (remember those days?).
