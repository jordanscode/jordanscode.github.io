Jordan Staniscia is a designer building digital products & services in San Francisco.

Currently, Jordan is a designer at Abstract, working on improving how designers actually design. It's admittedly, a bit meta.

Before that, Jordan was the lead designer of customer experience at Instacart, that company you might already get your groceries from every week. He's also worked at Storify and helped journalists tell stories with social media as platforms like Twitter and Facebook became newsworthy.
