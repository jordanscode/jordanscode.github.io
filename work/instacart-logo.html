Instacart Logo

Designing the iconic carrot for the next era of grocery delivery

I was sitting at my desk late one evening in 2015. I wasn’t super happy with the current state of the Instacart branding. I sketched out a concept of how we might simplify the mark, showed it to my desk mate, and then we both smiled. But it was late, and I was going to meet some friends for dinner. I closed my laptop and didn’t show anyone else again until 6 months later.
